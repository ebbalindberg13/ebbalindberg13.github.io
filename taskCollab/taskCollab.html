Overview
TaskCollab is an app for hybrid remote collaboration with a backlog feauture. This was part of my Graphical Interface course where the task was to create a video conferencing app that supported hybrid meetings. There are a lot of problems when having hybrid meetings, especially if people are connected with multiple devices in the meeting room. Continue reading to see how we solved the problem!


When
Autumn 2021
Team
Ebba Lindberg, Geoffrey Chen,
Norea Sandgren, Maja Ulen, Panpan Zhang
Methods
Sketching, wireframing, prototyping

Requirements

  • Video and audio calls
    Traditional options: name of the user, off/on mic and camera settings
  • Break-out roomsEnabling split meeting session rooms
  • Messages
    Both personal and group messages
  • Hybrid situation meetings
    Some people are together offline in the same room, and the others are distributed online
  • Live collaborative creation
    you can choose what kind of content (text, audio, pic, video)

For the live collaborative creation we chose to have a backlog.

Wire framing

A lot of time was spent on the ideation and wireframing to determine the best user flow.
